Industry Expertise

Deep Knowledge in the Markets That Matter

We don't just pull data — we understand the specific dynamics, regulations, and buyer behavior in each industry we serve.

Life Insurance & Medicare

The insurance industry has unique requirements that generic lead vendors don't understand. Agents are licensed by state and can only legally contact prospects in states where they hold an active license. Lead lists must respect this boundary.

We target prospects based on life-event triggers that correlate with insurance buying behavior: new homeowners carrying mortgage debt, new parents planning for their family's financial security, professionals at mid-career without adequate employer-sponsored coverage, and individuals approaching Medicare eligibility at age 65.

Every list is DNC-scrubbed and flagged with state-specific calling hour restrictions to keep your agency fully compliant.

B2B SaaS & Technology

SaaS sales cycles are driven by timing — reaching the right decision-maker when they're actively evaluating solutions. We leverage intent signals, hiring patterns, and technology stack data to identify companies that are in-market for your category.

Our targeting goes beyond basic firmographics. We filter by technology currently in use (to find companies using a competitor or an outdated tool), recent funding rounds (indicating budget availability), departmental headcount growth (signaling operational scaling), and job postings that describe the exact pain your product solves.

Leads are enriched with the context your SDRs need for relevant outreach: company size, growth stage, tech stack, and the specific role and seniority of each contact.

Recruiting & Staffing

Staffing agencies operate on two sides of the market simultaneously, and we build lead lists for both. On the client side, we identify hiring managers at companies with job postings that have been open for 30+ days — the signal that their internal recruiting efforts have stalled and they're ready to engage an agency.

On the candidate side, we track passive professionals who show signals of openness to new opportunities: recent LinkedIn profile updates, new certifications, role tenure beyond typical transition points, and activity patterns that suggest career exploration.

We filter by placement-fee-relevant industries — technology, healthcare, finance, and accounting — where each successful placement generates meaningful revenue for your agency.

Real Estate & Commercial Property

Commercial real estate decisions are made by a small group of people within each organization, and reaching them requires precision targeting. We identify facilities managers, operations directors, CFOs, and founders at companies showing physical expansion signals.

Growth indicators we track include headcount growth exceeding current office capacity, new market entry through remote job postings in unfamiliar geographies, lease expiration timing based on public filings, and recent funding that typically precedes physical expansion within 6-12 months.
