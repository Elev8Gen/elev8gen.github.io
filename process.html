Our Process

Five Stages From ICP to Inbox

A detailed look at every step of our pipeline — from understanding your ideal customer to delivering verified leads your team can act on today.

Stage 01

ICP Definition & Market Sizing

Every engagement begins with a deep understanding of who you're trying to reach. This isn't a form you fill out — it's a strategic conversation where we work together to define the precise characteristics of the prospects most likely to become your customers.

What We Define Together

  • Job titles and seniority levels — the specific roles that have buying authority or influence in the purchasing decision for your product or service
  • Industry and sub-industry — not just "insurance" but "independent life insurance agencies with 5-50 agents operating in the Southeast"
  • Company characteristics — employee count, annual revenue range, growth stage, technology stack, and geographic footprint
  • Behavioral signals — recent funding, active hiring in relevant departments, technology adoption patterns, and intent data indicating active evaluation of solutions in your category
  • Geographic boundaries — especially critical for regulated industries like insurance where state licensing determines your serviceable market

Market Sizing Report

Before we start any campaign, we run your ICP through our data infrastructure and provide a Total Addressable Market report. This tells you exactly how many prospects match your criteria across your target geography, so you can set realistic expectations and plan your sales capacity accordingly. We never promise more leads than actually exist in your market.

Stage 02

AI-Powered Prospect Discovery

With your ICP defined, our AI engines go to work across multiple data platforms simultaneously. This is where the scale and precision of our technology stack becomes your competitive advantage.

Discovery Engine

We use Clay's AI orchestration platform to coordinate searches across 150+ data providers, including Apollo.io's 275 million contact database. Clay's waterfall architecture means we're not limited to what any single provider knows — we query sequentially through multiple sources until we find every prospect that matches your criteria.

Signal Detection

Beyond static firmographic data, we monitor dynamic signals that indicate a prospect's likelihood of engagement:

  • Job change alerts — people who recently moved into a new role are 3x more likely to make purchasing decisions in their first 90 days
  • Company growth signals — headcount increases, new office openings, and departmental expansion
  • Technology adoption — recent additions or removals from a company's technology stack that create opportunity for complementary or replacement solutions
  • Funding events — recent investment rounds that correlate with increased spending on tools, services, and infrastructure

Deduplication & Cleanup

Raw prospect data inevitably contains duplicates, outdated entries, and contacts that fall outside your ICP on closer inspection. Our pipeline automatically deduplicates by identity (not just email), removes prospects who have left the company listed in the database, and filters out any contacts that don't meet your minimum criteria. What moves forward is a clean, deduplicated prospect list ready for enrichment.

Stage 03

Cross-Stack Data Enrichment

This is the core of what makes Elev8Gen different. Every prospect identified in Stage 2 is now enriched with the most accurate contact data available by querying multiple premium data providers and selecting the highest-confidence result for each data point.

How Cross-Stacking Works

For each prospect, our pipeline queries Apollo.io, Lusha, and additional specialized data providers to find their direct phone number, verified email address, current job title, and company information. When multiple providers return data for the same field, we compare the results and select the most recent, most frequently confirmed data point.

This approach solves the fundamental limitation of single-source data: no single database is 100% complete or 100% current. By cross-referencing across multiple independent sources, we achieve coverage and accuracy rates that no individual provider can match on their own.

Data Points We Enrich

Contact Level
  • Full name
  • Direct phone number (mobile preferred)
  • Verified work email
  • Current job title
  • Seniority level
  • Department
  • LinkedIn profile URL
  • Location (city, state)
Company Level
  • Company name
  • Industry and sub-industry
  • Employee count
  • Annual revenue range
  • Headquarters location
  • Website URL
  • Technology stack
  • Recent funding and growth signals
Stage 04

Multi-Layer Verification

Enrichment gets us the data. Verification confirms it's real. This is the quality gate that separates our leads from what traditional vendors deliver.

Phone Number Verification

Every phone number passes through Twilio's carrier-level Lookup API. This is the same infrastructure that powers phone verification for companies like Uber, Airbnb, and Stripe. For each number, we confirm:

  • Active status — is this number currently in service or has it been disconnected?
  • Line type — is it a mobile phone, landline, or VoIP number? Mobile numbers typically have the highest connect rates for outbound sales.
  • Carrier identification — which network operates this number? This helps identify potential VoIP or virtual numbers that may have lower connect rates.
  • Number validity — is the number format correct for its country and region? This catches data entry errors and incorrectly formatted international numbers.

Compliance Verification

After phone verification, every lead passes through our compliance engine:

  • Scrubbed against the National Do Not Call Registry — any number on the federal DNC list is automatically removed
  • Checked against state-specific regulations — some states have stricter calling hours (Florida, Massachusetts, Oklahoma, Washington, and Indiana enforce 8am-8pm instead of the federal 8am-9pm)
  • Flagged with calling hour windows in the recipient's timezone so your team knows exactly when each lead is legally callable

What Gets Filtered Out

Any lead that fails verification is removed before delivery. This includes disconnected numbers, numbers on the DNC registry, invalid formats, and numbers where carrier data indicates potential issues. You only receive leads that have passed every check in our pipeline.

Stage 05

Delivery & Ongoing Optimization

Verified leads reach your team in the format that fits your workflow, on the schedule that matches your sales capacity.

Delivery Options

  • CSV export — clean spreadsheets formatted for easy import into any system
  • Direct CRM integration — leads pushed automatically to Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, or your CRM of choice with all fields mapped correctly
  • Client dashboard — a live view of your lead pipeline, delivery history, and accuracy metrics accessible anytime through your browser
  • API access — for teams with custom systems, we provide programmatic access to your lead feed for real-time integration

Delivery Scheduling

Leads are delivered on the cadence that works for your team. Weekly batches are most common, but we support daily drip delivery for high-volume teams, monthly batches for smaller operations, and on-demand delivery for campaign-based work. The schedule is set based on your team's capacity to act on the leads — we'd rather deliver 200 leads your team can call this week than 800 that sit untouched for a month.

Continuous Optimization

The first batch is the starting point, not the finish line. As your team works through the leads, we analyze which profiles are converting and which aren't. We then refine the targeting — adjusting title keywords, company size ranges, geographic focus, or industry filters — to continuously improve the quality of each subsequent delivery. The best-performing campaigns we run have been through multiple optimization cycles and consistently outperform their first batch.

Technology Stack

The Tools Behind Our Pipeline

Clay

Orchestration & Waterfall Enrichment

Clay serves as the central orchestration layer of our pipeline. Its waterfall enrichment architecture allows us to query 150+ data providers sequentially for each prospect, maximizing coverage while minimizing cost. Clay's AI agent capabilities also enable custom research tasks that go beyond standard database lookups — analyzing LinkedIn activity, parsing company news, and identifying signals that structured databases miss.

Apollo.io

Primary Contact Database & Search

Apollo provides access to a database of over 275 million contacts with 65+ filterable attributes. We use Apollo's advanced search filters for initial prospect discovery — filtering by job title, seniority, industry, company size, technology stack, hiring activity, and funding events. Apollo's data serves as the foundation layer that subsequent enrichment providers build upon and verify.

Lusha

Premium Phone Data

Lusha specializes in direct dial phone numbers with particular strength in US and Western European markets. In our cross-stacking pipeline, Lusha frequently provides the phone number when Apollo's data is incomplete or when we need a second source to increase confidence. Lusha's data sourcing methodology produces consistently higher phone accuracy than most general-purpose contact databases.

Twilio Lookup

Carrier-Level Phone Verification

Twilio's Lookup API is the same carrier verification infrastructure trusted by companies like Uber, Airbnb, and Stripe. We use it as the final verification gate — every phone number in our pipeline is checked for active status, line type classification, and carrier identification before it's included in any deliverable. Numbers that fail this check are automatically removed.
