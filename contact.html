Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts required. We offer month-to-month engagements because we believe in earning your business through consistent quality, not locking you in.

How quickly can I receive my first leads?

Most campaigns deliver the first batch within 48 hours of finalizing the ICP. Complex or highly specialized targeting may take slightly longer.

What format do leads come in?

CSV by default. We also integrate directly with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and most major CRMs. Custom formats and API delivery are available for enterprise clients.

What if a lead has incorrect data?

We stand behind our verification process. If you receive leads with data that doesn't meet our quality standards, let us know and we'll investigate and make it right. Our goal is a long-term partnership, not a one-time transaction.

Do you work with companies outside the US?

Yes. While our verification infrastructure is strongest for US contacts, we serve clients globally and can build lead pipelines targeting prospects in North America, Europe, and other major markets.
